From the hasura community

Featured App

Faros Community Edition is an operational data platform for engineering teams that brings all engineering data in one place to give them a no-code/low-code BI, API, and automation layer for their operational data. Hasura is the API layer that connects Airbyte, n8n, and Metabase together. The stack lets you connect the entire chain from incident report through to code commit to see the full impact of your engineering efforts. They created their open-source version in just 4 weeks using Hasura. You can reach out to co-founder Thomas Gerber#5893 on our Discord channel if you want to know more about how they built Faros or use it for yourself.

Top OSS Contributions of the Month

  • Teo Stocco recently submitted a PR to the GraphQL Engine server that lets you cast the JSONB column to text. You can now apply full-text operators to JSONB columns in Postgres.
  • Chris Vibert added TypeScript support to our ra-data-hasura project!

Monthly Recommendation

This month’s recommendation comes from our field marketing manager, Olivia.

Did you know that in Miami, a center of the exotic pet trade, dealers used to import them from Southeast Asia by the tens of thousands? At some point, python owners who no longer wanted to care for them let them go in the Everglades. By the mid-1990s, the pythons had established a breeding population. No one knows how many pythons are out there now but estimates run from 10,000 to perhaps hundreds of thousands. That's why I love Guardians of the Glades. It's a truly bonkers show that follows a group of python hunters who are looking to remove the invasive species from the Glades.
